The Best Movies According to RJ

My top 4 movies of all-time.

Titanic

Based on the true tragic story of RMS Titanic that sunk in 1912
when it struck an iceberg.

3 Idiots

Highly recommended movie for students who are embarking the journey in
pursuing their college degree.

Baahubali: The Beginning

An action packed fantasy movie following the drama on who would be the rightful
successor of the throne.

Baahubali 2: The Conclusion

Just like the part one, this movie uncovers the twist that everyone would'nt expect.
